Career Overview

The Objective of Security Services is to prevent crime against people, property, and information. Some of the responsibilities that come with this job is installing security equipment such as cameras, intercoms, card readers, etc. With this you also have the responsibility of servicing said equipment to make sure it stays up and running 24/7. People in this industry also have the responsibility of being the security project manager which is responsible for coordinating where the equipment is going to be placed. The impact it has on society is keeping people safe as well as their property.

Education Requirements

Most people in the security services attend a vocational school or have an associates degree. The necessary skills for this job are IT experience, programming skills, math skills, and the ability to interact well with clients. Some programs people take are telecommunications and electrical. When it comes to what school people in this field go to, as stated before a lot go to vocational schools or attend programs where they learn these skills.
